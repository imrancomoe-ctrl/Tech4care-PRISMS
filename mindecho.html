MindEcho

AI-powered app that assesses teen mental health through voice and emotion analysis.

The adolescent mental health crisis has become one of the most pressing issues in the United States today. MindEcho is a mobile application designed specifically for adolescents. Rather than relying on lengthy questionnaires, it conducts a 5-minute guided voice interaction that uses artificial intelligence to simultaneously analyze what the user says (semantic content via NLP) and how they say it (acoustic features via Speech Emotion Recognition). The result is an objective, multi-dimensional stress level assessment.
