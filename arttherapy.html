Tech4Care Prisms

Art therapy

Turning drawing into a communication tool for nonverbal autistic children.

The communication barriers faced by nonverbal autistic children highlight the need for more accessible and adaptive methods of expression. This project focuses on teaching nonverbal autistic children how to draw as a means of communication by observing their interactions and providing guidance when needed. The system begins with simple, familiar objects and gradually progresses to more complex drawing tasks, supporting skill development over time. By monitoring user engagement and offering targeted direction, it adapts to individual learning needs while encouraging independent expression. The resulting drawings serve as a functional communication tool, enabling children to convey their needs, ideas, and emotions without relying on verbal language.
