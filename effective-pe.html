Effective PE Teaching Methods for Autistic Individuals

This project focuses on developing and evaluating effective physical education (PE) teaching methods tailored for autistic individuals.

Physical education plays an important role in supporting the physical, social, and emotional development of autistic individuals. Because autistic individuals may experience differences in communication, sensory processing, motor coordination, and social interaction. PE teachers need exclusive teaching methods that match their needs suitably. Basketball can serve as a useful teaching medium because it offers structured, repeatable skills such as dribbling, passing, and shooting, while also encouraging teamwork, turn-taking, focus, and body coordination. With proper adaptations, clear instructions, emotional support, and positive reinforcement, basketball activities can help autistic individuals participate more confidently and meaningfully in physical education.
